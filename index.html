College Information

Sanjivani College of Engineering, Kopargaon is a leading autonomous institute established in 1983, affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University. With NAAC ‘A’ Grade and NBA accreditation, the college offers strong B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA programs supported by modern labs, hostels, sports facilities, and vibrant student activities. Sanjivani is known for innovation, research culture, and solid placements with companies like TCS and L&T.
